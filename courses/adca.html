Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA)

The Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA) is a professionally crafted 12-month program designed to provide in-depth knowledge of essential computer technologies, programming languages, productivity tools, and modern-day applications. Starting with the fundamentals of computer hardware, software, networks, operating systems, and basic security, the course ensures a strong foundation for anyone stepping into the world of digital technology.

As the course progresses, students gain hands-on experience with Microsoft Office tools such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and even AI-based tools like ChatGPT and Copilot for productivity enhancement. Core programming modules in Python and C/C++ teach logic building, object-oriented programming, and error handling. Students also learn full front-end web development using HTML and CSS, along with server-side scripting using PHP and MySQL, enabling them to build responsive and secure web applications. Additionally, comprehensive training in database management systems, including advanced SQL and NoSQL concepts, is provided.

A major highlight of this course is the inclusion of Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence, and Accounting in Tally Prime. Students are introduced to cybersecurity best practices, data privacy, cryptography, and threat mitigation strategies. They also explore AI tools and their real-world applications, preparing them for future tech advancements. The course concludes with professional accounting training in Tally Prime, covering company setup, ledger maintenance, GST compliance, and inventory control—making students well-rounded and job-ready for multiple IT and business domains.

Course Requirements

To get the most out of the Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications (ADCA) course at The Geek Institute of Cyber Security, students should meet the following requirements:

    01. Educational Qualification
  • Minimum: 10th Pass (Matriculation) from a recognized board
  • Preferred: 12th Pass or equivalent for better understanding of advanced modules
    • 02. Willingness to Learn
  • A strong motivation to learn digital tools, programming, and modern technology
  • Regular attendance and active participation in assignments and projects

Professional Experience and Skills Gained from ADCA Course

Upon completing the Advanced Diploma in Computer Applications, students acquire a solid blend of practical and theoretical skills essential for the modern IT workplace. Graduates become proficient in using key productivity software such as Microsoft Office Suite and AI-powered tools like ChatGPT and Copilot, enabling them to efficiently manage documents, data analysis, presentations, and communications in a professional environment.

  • Proficient use of Microsoft Office and AI tools like ChatGPT and Copilot for efficient document creation, data analysis, presentations, and communication in professional settings.
  • Strong programming skills in Python and C/C++, enabling problem-solving, software development, and debugging across various applications.
  • Hands-on experience in web development including front-end (HTML, CSS) and back-end (PHP, MySQL), allowing creation and management of dynamic, secure websites and applications.
  • Fundamental knowledge of cybersecurity including threat identification, secure coding practices, cryptography, and data privacy, preparing students to protect digital assets effectively.
  • Expertise in Tally Prime accounting software, covering company setup, ledger management, GST compliance, and inventory control, useful for finance and business operations roles.
