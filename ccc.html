Course on Computer Concepts (CCC)

Course Description:

The "Course on Computer Concepts" (CCC) offers a foundational understanding of key computer principles. This program covers essential topics such as hardware, software, operating systems, and basic digital literacy. Participants will gain insights into computer functionality, file management, and online communication tools. Through interactive learning, students will develop practical skills in word processing, spreadsheet management, and internet navigation. The CCC course aims to empower individuals with a solid grasp of fundamental computer concepts, enabling them to confidently engage with technology in personal and professional spheres. Upon completion, participants will possess the knowledge needed to effectively utilize digital tools and enhance their digital presence.

Understanding computer hardware and software.
Overview of operating systems and their functions.
Navigating the user interface: desktop, icons, taskbar.

Introduction to word processing software.
Creating, saving, and opening documents.
ormatting text: fonts, styles, alignment.
Adding images, tables, and headers/footers.

Introduction to spreadsheets and spreadsheet software.
Creating, saving, and opening spreadsheets.
Data entry and formatting.
Basic formulas and functions.

Browsing the internet using web browsers.
Conducting online searches effectively.
Evaluating website credibility.
Basics of online communication and social media.

Setting up and managing email accounts.
Composing, sending, and receiving emails.
Organizing emails into folders.
Attaching files to emails.

Understanding the importance of computer security.
Basic measures for protecting personal data.
Recognizing and avoiding common online threats.

Using computers for personal finance management.
Creating digital presentations.
Basic troubleshooting and technical support.

Review of key concepts from each module.
Practical assignments to apply learned skills.
Final examination covering all modules.
Course Objectives:

By the end of this program, students should be able to:

  • Demonstrate an understanding of fundamental computer concepts and terminologies.

  • Navigate and effectively use various components of an operating system.

  • Create, edit, and format documents using word processing software.

  • Create, format, and manage data in spreadsheets.

  • Utilize the internet for browsing, searching, and online communication.

  • Compose and manage emails efficiently.

  • Apply computer skills for personal, academic, and professional tasks.
